How It Works

A simple, secure flow from capture to co-ownership.

  1. Step 1

    Capture / Generate

    Upload or AI-generate on-brand portraits from approved templates and styles.

  2. Step 2

    Encrypt & Store Off-Chain

    Images stored encrypted; only hashed proofs and metadata go on-chain.

  3. Step 3

    Mint NFT & Set Rights

    Smart contracts define usage during employment and alumni phases.

  4. Step 4

    Integrate & Govern

    Sync to HRIS/ID/IAM. Consent, audit trails, and revocation handled from a single admin panel.

FAQs

Do you store faces on-chain?

No. Images are encrypted off-chain; only hashes/metadata are on-chain for integrity and provenance.

Which blockchains are supported?

Configurable: enterprise EVM chains (Polygon, Base, private EVM), Flow, or permissioned ledgers per your compliance needs.

How do alumni rights work?

Smart contracts define post-employment rights, including retention, licensing back to the company, or transfer to the individual.
